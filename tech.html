Slippery Liquid Infused Porous Surface

Non-stick, ultra repellent self-healing, surface coatings for industrial and medical materials.

Inspired by the Nepenthes pitcher plant, provides unique capabilities that are unmatched by any other liquid repellent surface technologies.

This inexpensive super repellent surface has grabbed the sectors ranging from refrigeration & architecture to consumer products. The repellent surfaces designed is based after lotus leaves which are extremely hydrophobic due to their rough, wary surface, has no far met the mark: they may repel water but fail to repel oils and others.

SLIPS technology inspired by the slippery pitcher plant can be applied to wider range of materials including plastics, metals, ceramics, glass and even concrete. They repel all kind of liquids and products: oil, paint, emulsions, lotions and gels, as well as blood, bacteria and marine organisms.

The versatility of SLIPS, their robustness and unique ability to selfheal makes it possible to design these surfaces for use almost anywhere, even under extreme temperature and pressure conditions.
